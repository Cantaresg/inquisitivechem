How It Works

A real lab, in your browser.

01
🧫
Measure your chemicals

Use a virtual measuring cylinder — click to set the volume, read the graduation marks, pour into the reaction vessel.

02
⏱️
Start the stopwatch yourself

You decide when to start timing — just like in a real experiment. Or enable auto-start to begin the moment the chemicals mix.

03
🌡️
Control temperature

Set the water bath temperature before running the experiment and observe how it changes the reaction rate.

04
📊
Record and analyse

Log your results in the built-in data table. Spot patterns, draw conclusions, and answer the guided questions.
